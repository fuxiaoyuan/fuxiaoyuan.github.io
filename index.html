About me

I received the Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2019. I am currently working as an associate research fellow at the State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology, BUPT. My current research interests include edge computing, resource management, and deep reinforcement learning.
